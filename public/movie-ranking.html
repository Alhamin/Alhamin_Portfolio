The Best Movies According to Alhamin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Saworoide

Saworoide is a Yoruba movie released in 1999 and directed by Tunde Kelani. The film is set in the pre-colonial era and explores themes of culture, tradition, and politics in a typical Yoruba kingdom.

Ibi Giga

Ibi giga is a very old film produced around 2009. That is 11 years ago. Segun Ogungbe is the major role here starring Odunlade Adekola, Fathia Balogun and so on. Segun Ogungbe was a very poor type, poorer than a church rat. His wife had always complained of his poorness. He has a daughter of around 10 years old.


In summary, his wife left him for a rich husband and later he became successful. The road to success is not smooth. Watch out this interesting Nollywood movie and see!

Ayinla

Ayinla is a movie based on the life and career of Ayinla Omowura, a popular Yoruba Apala musician. This film might provide an in-depth look at his music career, his life's ups and downs, and his impact on the Yoruba music scene.
